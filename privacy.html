Privacy Policy

AI Data Shield — Chrome Extension

Effective Date: June 8, 2026
Last Updated: June 8, 2026

Summary: AI Data Shield processes all data 100% locally on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Ever.

1. Who We Are

AI Data Shield is a Chrome browser extension developed to protect sensitive personal information from being accidentally submitted to AI platforms such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Microsoft Copilot.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identifiable information (names, SSNs, emails, phone numbers, etc.)
  • The content of your messages or documents
  • Browsing history or activity
  • Financial or medical information
  • Any data from your device

3. How the Extension Works

All processing happens exclusively on your local device:

  • The extension scans text you are about to send to an AI platform
  • Sensitive data patterns are detected using locally-run pattern matching
  • Detected data is replaced with anonymous tokens before transmission
  • A local audit log is stored in your browser's local storage only
  • No data is ever sent to our servers — because we have none

4. Permissions Used

  • activeTab: To scan and redact text in the active browser tab on supported AI platforms only
  • storage: To save your settings (Strict Mode, Stealth Mode) and a local audit log on your device only
  • clipboardWrite: To copy redacted (safe) text to your clipboard when you use the Shield panel

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core function. No data leaves your device.

5. Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's chrome.storage.local to store:

  • Your preference settings (Strict Mode on/off, Stealth Mode on/off)
  • A rolling audit log of up to 100 recent redaction events (site name, timestamp, types of data detected — never the actual sensitive data)

This data lives entirely on your device and can be cleared at any time using the "Clear Active Session Vault" button in the extension.

6. Third-Party Services

AI Data Shield does not integrate with, communicate with, or share data with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

7. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
